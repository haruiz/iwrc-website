International Weed Recognition Consortium

A shared home for weed recognition research and resources.

IWRC promotes the development and use of sensor-based weed recognition capability for advanced weed research and precision weed control applications. The site is organized for researchers, data contributors, and precision agriculture teams that need protocols, projects, datasets, and consortium contacts.

Consortium Focus

From field images to reliable recognition systems

IWRC centers the practical pieces required for machine-learning weed recognition: image collection, annotation, datasets, model training, discovery, and outreach.

Vision Statement

Promote sensor-based weed recognition for research and precision control.

IWRC frames weed recognition as a community challenge: useful tools require shared field practices, high-quality annotated imagery, accessible datasets, and training that helps researchers apply the technology responsibly.
